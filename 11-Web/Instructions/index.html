Project Summary: Latitude's Influence

Weather across the globe varies widely. Temperature, humidity, cloud coverage, and wind speed all factor into how weather in different cities feel. The humidity in NC can make an 80 degree day feel a lot hotter than an 80 degree day on the west coast, where humidity is less of a factor. What if we compare two cities closer or farther to the equator? Are maximum temperatures for both cities relatively the same, or do they vary? What about cloud coverage, humidity, and wind speed?

To understand the differences in weather across the globe, a random sample of cities were chosen using latitudinal and longitudinal coordinates to allow a comparison of maximum temperature, humidity, cloud coverage, and wind speed.

The Plots tab will take you to each individual analysis, while the Comparisons page will let you look at all four weather conditions next to each other. Check out Dive into the Data to see the cities chosen!
